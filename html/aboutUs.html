What Motivates Us

Avick, part of the Maxi Mobility holding company, connects private and business users with the forms of transport that best suit their needs. With the aim of making cities better places to live, we’ve created a sustainable business model guided by ethical principles to help us act responsibly. More than simply ‘mobility as a service’ (MaaS), we seek to offer a future full of new possibilities for the 40+ cities in which we operate, respecting both the environment and the people that live there.
